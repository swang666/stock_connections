# AI Server MLCC Demand

> **Demand driver.** AI/GP servers consume large quantities of high-capacitance, large-form-factor MLCCs (multilayer ceramic capacitors); design-in counts and specs are rising sharply per AI accelerator.

**What it is:** Server-grade MLCC demand pulling industry supply/demand into a multi-year shortage (J.P. Morgan models ~6% shortage by 2028E vs 10% oversupply in 2025), with high-50% ASP growth and limited effective-capacity additions (yield/trade-loss penalties on larger, ultra-high-capacitance parts for AI servers).

## Companies that benefit
- [[Samsung Electro-Mechanics]] — (VIEW, J.P. Morgan 2026-06-12): AI-server-grade MLCC design wins; capacity booked/tight; multi-year shortage from 2027
- [[Sinocera]] — (VIEW, J.P. Morgan 2026-06-12): upstream MLCC dielectric powder into the AI-server MLCC shortage
- Global leaders cited (no node yet): Murata, Taiyo Yuden, TDK, Yageo, Walsin — JPM prefers JP/TW names over KR on valuation.

## Constraint / bottleneck signals
- Supply shortage through 2028E; capacity allocation requests surging; effective capacity constrained by yield/trade-loss on larger AI-server parts; ASP +high-50%.
