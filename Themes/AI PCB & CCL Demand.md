# AI PCB & CCL Demand

> **Demand driver.** AI servers and switches drive a step-change in printed-circuit-board (PCB) layer counts and copper-clad-laminate (CCL) material grades, expanding both unit volume and ASP across the PCB/CCL/substrate stack.

**What it is:** AI PCB TAM modeled to roughly double for two years (≈$5.6bn 2025 → $12.2bn 2026 → $25.5bn 2027). PCB layer counts rise from low-double-digits to 40+ (some 78-layer) designs; higher-grade CCL (M8/M9), electronic glass cloth, copper foil and drill bits see amplified demand. Persistent CCL/substrate supply shortage is pushing ASPs up (Kingboard ~+50-60% cumulative since 2025).

## Demand driver
- [[NVIDIA]] drives demand for this theme — (VIEW/EST, JPM/Jefferies 2026-06): Nvidia AI CCL demand ~1.5m sheets/mo by 2027; ASIC 2.0-2.5m sheets/mo

## Constraint / bottleneck signals
- CCL supply shortage across grades; high-end capacity (M8/M9) constrained by yield; supply could undershoot demand; ASPs rising via repeated price hikes.
