# Server CPU & Agentic AI Demand

> **Demand driver.** Agentic AI shifts the CPU:GPU ratio toward 1:1, expanding the server-CPU TAM.

**What it is:** The shift from gen-AI 1.0 (chatbot) to 2.0 (agent) raises CPU workload vs GPU, lifting the server-CPU CPU:GPU ratio from ~1:4/1:8 toward 1:1+. Bernstein raises the 2030 server-CPU TAM to ~$223bn (6x 2025).
